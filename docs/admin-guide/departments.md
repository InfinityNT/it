# Departments

Departments represent organizational units within your company. They are used to group employees and can be referenced in reports and filters.

## Viewing Departments

Departments are visible in employee records and filter options. To manage departments, use the **Manage Departments** dialog from the Employees page.

## Adding a Department

1. Open **Manage Departments** from the Employees page
2. Click **Add Department**
3. Enter the department name
4. Optionally designate a department responsible person
5. Save the new department

## Editing a Department

1. Open **Manage Departments**
2. Click the edit icon next to the department
3. Update the name or responsible person
4. Save changes

## Department Responsible

Each department can have a designated responsible person. This person may receive notifications about assignments and approvals related to their department's employees.

## Deleting a Department

Departments can only be deleted if no employees are currently assigned to them. If employees exist in a department, reassign them first before deleting.
