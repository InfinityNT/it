# Employees

The Employees section manages records for people who can be assigned devices.

## Employee List

The employee list displays:

- **Name** — Employee's full name
- **Department** — Their organizational department
- **Job Title** — Their role or position
- **Status** — Active or inactive
- **Assigned Devices** — Number of devices currently assigned

## Filtering

Filter employees by:

- **Department** — Select a specific department
- **Status** — Active or inactive employees
- **Search** — Search by name, email, or employee ID

## Employee Details

Click an employee to see their full profile:

- Contact information
- Department and job title
- List of currently assigned devices
- Full assignment history

## Adding an Employee

Users with the appropriate permissions can add new employees by providing:

1. First and last name
2. Email address
3. Department and job title
4. Any additional notes

Employees can optionally be linked to a system user account for login access.
